About Smart Local

Smart Local is a technology-driven civic platform built to solve everyday local problems across India. We help communities report and track issues like air pollution, waste disposal, water logging, lost items, and safety alerts in one smart digital space.

By combining modern technology with community participation, Smart Local transforms local voices into real-world solutions—creating cleaner, safer, and smarter neighborhoods.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower citizens with smart technology that enables them to report, track, and resolve local issues efficiently—building responsible and sustainable communities across India.

🌱 Our Vision

To create a future where every Indian neighborhood is smart, responsive, and transparent—driven by technology, trust, and community action.
